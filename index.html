SEMATARY GRAVE MAN

BY BONO SORONZONBOLD

SEMATARY (born December 22, 2000), also known as SEMATARY GRAVE MAN FROM THE HAUNTED MOUND REAL NAZGUL SKINWALKER KEEPER OF DA TREES HAUNTED MOUND LORD, or DJ SORROW, is an American rapper, songwriter, and record producer. He began releasing music and founded the Haunted Mound rap collective in 2019. Sematary has released eight mixtapes and three EPs. His distorted and overblown production style, which is influenced by witch house, black metal and his deep-fried aesthetic have garnered him a cult following online.

"Grave House" is a 12 track mixtape and is the first release of Sematary's discography. It is most notable for its grungy and washed out trap beats, deadpan vocals, esoteric lyricism, and its use of samples from black metal songs. This mixtape features verses on every song from co-rapper, GHOST MOUNTAIN, providing his own unique voice and lyrics.
